About Saikiran Sondarkar

Saikiran Sondarkar is an inspiring educator, tech leader, and entrepreneur, best known as the Founder & CEO of LetsUpgrade EdTech, a prominent Indian platform dedicated to upskilling engineers. Currently serving as the Program Director for B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering at ITM Skills University, Saikiran is deeply passionate about fostering learning and innovation within the tech community. His journey includes notable achievements as a hackathon winner and prior roles at ITM Group of Institutions and Yobykesev.

  • His vision and dedication in founding and growing LetsUpgrade, making quality tech education accessible to a vast community of students.
  • His commitment to education, evident in his role at ITM Skills University and his passion for upskilling the next generation of engineers.
  • His entrepreneurial spirit and ability to identify and solve problems, as demonstrated by his various ventures and hackathon successes.
  • His active engagement with the tech community and his role as a speaker and mentor, inspiring others in the field.
